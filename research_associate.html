Dr. Arash Nouri

Dr. Arash G. Nouri is a Research Scientist at the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science in Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh (Pitt). He received his Ph.D. degree in computational modeling and simulation (CMS) from Pitt, his M.S. degree in chemical engineering also from Pitt, and his M.S. and B.S. degrees in mechanical engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

Dr. Nikita Gourianov

Dr. Nikita Gourianov recently finished his D.Phil. in Atomic & Laser Physics at Keble College, Oxford University with a thesis on the utility of tensor networks methods for turbulence simulations. Through this experience, he has gained expertise in tensor networks, high-dimensional optimization, data compression algorithms, low-level numerical linear algebra, partial differential equations and quantum computing. His interests largely lie in computational science, finance and commercialization of technologies.
